Om løpet:

All info på denne siden er basert på åpen kildekode. Vi gjør vårt beste for å holde den oppdatert, men kan ikke garantere at alt er korrekt. Ser du noen feil, vennligst kontakt oss på kontakt så retter vi det så snart som mulig.

Get ready for an exhilarating running experience at the Norgesløpet race on September 28, 2024 in Jessheim! Whether you're a seasoned runner or just starting out, this event has something for everyone. With race categories including a 5 km and a half marathon, participants can choose the distance that fits their fitness level and goals. The race will take place on a scenic road course, providing runners with breathtaking views and a challenging yet rewarding route. As the race director, I am excited to welcome all participants to this unforgettable event. With over 18,000 photos captured throughout the race, you'll have plenty of memories to look back on. So lace up your running shoes, gather your friends and family, and join us for a day filled with friendly competition and a sense of accomplishment. Whether you're aiming for a personal best or simply want to enjoy a fun run, Norgesløpet promises to be an event to remember. We can't wait to see you at the finish line!
