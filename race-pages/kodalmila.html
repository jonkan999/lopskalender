Om løpet:

All info på denne siden er basert på åpen kildekode. Vi gjør vårt beste for å holde den oppdatert, men kan ikke garantere at alt er korrekt. Ser du noen feil, vennligst kontakt oss på kontakt så retter vi det så snart som mulig.

Sandefjords Blad is excited to announce the annual Kodalmila race, featuring the 5 km and 10 km road race categories. Runners of all levels are invited to participate in this thrilling event, which will take place in the beautiful city of Sandefjord. The race will offer participants the chance to challenge themselves, whether they are seasoned runners or new to the sport. Kodalmila will also cover aktuelle temaer, giving runners the opportunity to engage with current topics while enjoying the thrill of the race. The race will be featured in our papiravisen (eAvis), allowing participants and spectators to stay up to date with all the latest news and information. With the stunning scenery of Sandefjord as a backdrop, runners can expect a memorable and enjoyable experience. In addition to the competitive aspect of the race, participants will have the opportunity to connect with fellow runners and communities, making it a truly unique and enriching experience. Come join us for Kodalmila, a race that promises to be an unforgettable and fulfilling event for all involved.
